Definition:
Raster graphics are images made up of a **grid of individual pixels**, where each pixel stores a specific color value.

### **Core Idea**

- Raster image is divided into tiny squares (pixels)
- Each pixel is colored
- When viewed together, they form image
- Resolution Dependent

When zooming in far enough, image will become pixelated and blurry

### **Common Raster Formats**

- JPEG (.jpg)
- PNG (.png)
- GIF (.gif)
- BMP (.bmp)

### **Where Raster Graphics Are Used**

- Photos
- videos
- Game framebuffers
- Screen Displays
